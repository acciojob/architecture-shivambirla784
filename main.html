Information Architecture (IA) is the practice of structuring and presenting the parts of something - whether that's a website, mobile app, blog post, book, or brick-and-mortar store - to users so that it's easy to understand.
For the user to be able to make sense of conten, structure it with two major factors in mind: the user's context and the user's needs.

Context is the environment in which the user engages with the content. To figure out the context, consider where, when, why, and how the user is seeking out and engaging with your content.
